It's just a timer app, but it is the best timer app.

- In most timer apps, it takes about 6 keypresses to make a new timer and start it. Here, it takes just 1 or 2.

- It's designed to be comfortably usable one-handed. Everything interactive sits at the bottom half of the phone.

- In most apps, alarms will keep sounding until the user gets their phone out and interacts with the app. For many users, that's just an inconvenience, so we make it optional. Our alarms ring once and then shut up, by default.

- The timers are visually compact and color-coded, so you can easily find and reuse the timers you'll need often.

As far as we're aware, no other timer app has any of these qualities, let alone all of them.

It also has a special feature called "timercules". Timercules are composite timers that other timers can be dragged into. There are a few different kinds. "Sequence" timercules can be used for timing multi-stage cooking processes, or complex exercise routines. "Cycle" timercules can repeat, which can be used to make, for instance, pomodoro timers. Parallel timercules are a bit more obscure, but if you experiment a lot with recipes, you'll find a use for them.

And of course, we do have stopwatches.

It's also just beautiful. There are no superfluous details. Every change animates smoothly, for visual clarity.

It's available on both android and ios, right now. It's free to try.
